Here is a list of major Dentitox Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Vitamin A

Vitamin A is a crucial ingredient in Dentitox Pro. It plays a significant role in maintaining the health of our teeth and gums. It aids in the formation of dentin, a layer of hard material just below the surface of the teeth, contributing to stronger and healthier teeth.
2

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is essential for the production of collagen, a protein that helps fight periodontal disease. It strengthens the gums and the soft tissue in your mouth. It can protect against gingivitis, the early stage of gum disease, and can prevent your teeth from loosening.
3

Vitamin D3

Vitamin D3 is another vital ingredient in Dentitox Pro. It helps the body absorb calcium and phosphate, nutrients that are necessary for healthy teeth and bones. It can also help to reduce inflammation and fight bacterial infections in the mouth.
4

Calcium

Calcium is one of the most important minerals for oral health. It strengthens the enamel, the hard outer surface of the teeth, and helps prevent tooth decay. It also plays a role in maintaining healthy gums and jawbone.
5

Potassium

Potassium is a key mineral that works alongside magnesium to prevent blood from becoming too acidic, which can leach calcium from your bones and teeth. It also helps to maintain the density of your teeth and keeps the surrounding gum tissue healthy.
6

Phosphorus

Phosphorus is essential for oral health, as it helps to maintain the strength and integrity of teeth and jawbones. It works with calcium to build strong teeth and prevent oral health problems like tooth decay and gum disease.
7

Zinc

Zinc is a trace mineral that plays a vital role in oral health. It helps to prevent the growth of bacteria and plaque along the gum line and on the surfaces of the teeth. It also promotes wound healing and can help to prevent and treat gum disease.
8

Iron

Iron is an essential mineral that ensures the health of your teeth and gums. It helps to keep your tongue healthy and protects your gums from diseases. Iron deficiency can lead to oral health problems like inflamed tongue and mouth sores.
9

Elderberry Extract

Elderberry Extract is a natural ingredient known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It can help to reduce inflammation in the gums and prevent oral infections.
10

Peppermint

Peppermint is often used in oral health products for its refreshing flavor and its ability to kill bacteria that can cause gum disease and tooth decay. It also provides a cooling effect and can help to soothe inflamed gums.
